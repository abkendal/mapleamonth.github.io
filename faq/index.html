Maple-a-Month FAQ


Can I cancel at any time?

Yes. You can cancel any time you choose. You will get the packages that you have already paid for and you will not be charged any further.

How do I cancel my subscription?

  • Click on “Details” on one of your payments to Maple-a-Month on paypal.com
  • Click the top link that says “in reference to subscription xxx”
  • Click the cancel button at the bottom of the page

What countries do you ship to?

We ship worldwide (where law allows). Maple syrup for all!

How do I use maple syrup for cooking?

Pure maple syrup can be used as a healthy and flavourful substitute for sugar in any entree or dessert. The rich flavor adds depth and complexity to all your favourite dishes. Use it as a sweetener in rubs, glazes, sauces, cookies and cakes.

When will I receive my delivery?

All orders must be in before the end of the month. Deliveries for the month will be sent on the 2-3rd of the next month.

I would like to review Maple a Month in my blog / youTube channel / news site…

We will consider all review sample requests. Please send any requests to info@mapleamonth.com

Send us an e-mail at info@mapleamonth.com
